Your Gateway to Sardinia's Most Iconic Locations

From the moment you touch down at Olbia Costa Smeralda Airport or arrive at the Porto Isola Bianca, I ensure a seamless transfer to the island's most researched and prestigious destinations. Whether you are heading to a private villa or a world-class resort, I specialize in transfers to:

Porto Cervo & Porto Rotondo

The crown jewels of the Costa Smeralda, home to luxury yachts and elite boutiques.

Cagliari & Alghero

Long-distance premium transfers across the island in total relaxation.

Palau & La Maddalena

The gateway to the breathtaking archipelago and the iconic Bear's Rock.
